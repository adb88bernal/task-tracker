How Task Tracker Works

       Task Tracker helps you keeps track of your one-time, weekly, or monthly tasks in order to librate your mind from remembering often overlooked jobs. You're allowed to set up weekly, one-time, and monthly tasks.

       Depending on the type of task you choose, already completed weekly or monthly task will be in the trash repository but will reappear at the beginning of the week (Monday) or month (1st of the month). You are allowed to modify your task settings to best suit your needs.
